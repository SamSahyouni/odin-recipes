Aunt Mona's Knafeh

There is no knafeh that compares to my Aunt Mona's knafeh at Christmas. And now my sister Hannah and I make it too!

Ingredients

  • Shredded philo dough
  • Butter
  • Sweet cheese
  • Simple Syrup
  • Rose Water

Recipe

  1. Shred the shredded philo dough
  2. Melt butter in a large pan and lightly brown the philo dough in the butter
  3. Butter the cooking pan
  4. Layer the bottom of the pan with the browned philo dough
  5. Layer the sweet cheese on top of the first layer
  6. Brown the second half of the philo dough and layer it on top of the dish
  7. Bake in the oven
  8. Serve with syrup to taste
